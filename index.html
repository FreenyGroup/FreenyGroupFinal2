About Us

Where we started, and where were going

Initially starting as a local vehicle rental company The Freeny Group has grown beyond just handing the keys. Beggining with DMV CAB Corporation, where we provided underserved students with transportation, The Freeny Group has continued with the idea of providing the ultimate transportation experience, be it from school, to work, or across the country, count on the Freeny Group and its sister companies to get you there!

The Freeny Group began as a local transportation company named DMV CAB Corporation, a locally operated company in the Washingtn D.C., Virginia, and Maryland area, where our main priority consisted of providing underserved, or students with disabilities transportation to and from their school of origin.

Over the years we have slowly grown into a multifaceted organization that operates in many parts, offering many unique services.

Our mission at Freeny Group is to provide seamless vehicles, transportation, and education through our sister companies in a caring, thoughtful, and respectable manner.
